LGBTQ+ Rights

What does it mean?

LGBTQ+ Rights encompass how openly they can live without discrimination surrounding their sexuality and who they are with equal rights allowing them to express themselves freely.

Why are LGBTQ+ Rights So Important?

LGBTQ+ Rights are so important for people apart of that community to feel safe no matter where they go. Equal rights for them allows them to feel respected and feel safe to express themselves freely.It would protect them from any discrimintation towards them. People are people and love is love. There are countries where same sex marraige is illegal. It's important to let people change their appearances, express themselves, and even love who they want to love. There shouldn't be any hatred or judgement towards anyone who want to love who they want to love or who want to transition to another gender because they don't feel right in their "biological gender".

"A lifelong Republican born and raised in deep-red East Texas, Thomas Smith often laments that he felt forced to become a Democrat this year.

“Ever since the Texas GOP put out their platform earlier in the year, I feel like they deliberately excluded me from the party,” he said. “I don’t agree with all Democratic ideas, but I would rather be part of them than support a group of people who personally have a vendetta against myself.”

Smith, a 52-year-old former police officer who lives in Livingston and objects to the Texas Republican Party’s stance on same-sex marriage, is growing increasingly concerned about his rights as a gay man ahead of the midterm elections and 2023 legislative session." (www.texastribune.org)
